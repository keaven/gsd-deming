5 Overview

We consider other alternative tests for group sequential design.

  • Weighted logrank tests such as the Harrington and Fleming (1982) and Magirr and Burman (2019a) tests.
  • Combination tests such as the MaxCombo test by Lin et al (2020), Roychoudhury et al (2021).

We will illustrate the concepts based on an experimental R packages.

  • gsdmvn: analytically based group sequential design.

In this part, we will cover

  • Review of fixed design based on weighted logrank test and MaxCombo test
  • Group sequential design with weighted logrank test
  • Group sequential design with MaxCombo test
